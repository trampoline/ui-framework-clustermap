Mapping Europe’s startup ecosystem

This platform is being developed as part of Startup Europe to provide a detailed and consistent analysis of the startup ecosystem across Europe. New functionality and data sources are being introduced progressively through 2015 and 2016. The current beta release contains only partial data and some known inconsistencies.
